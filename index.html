Sci-Fi books

Cataloging science fiction into different subgenres can be as complex as defining what science fiction is as such. The characteristics that define each one of the divisions can share characteristics with another branch, something capable of confusing when we want to refer to a specific subgenus. Borders change over time, so it's best to be flexible with categories.

The classification presented below is not a simple list, but rather a series of topics in which science fiction can be grouped around a central rule. Each novel belongs to several subgenres. I think that these divisions can increase to infinity, although the groups that I propose seem sufficient to me.
