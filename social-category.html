Social Sci-Fi

The awareness of the human race

They are works that explore possible scenarios for the future or past of humanity, focusing on different types of societies. They place special emphasis on individual freedoms, class struggle, ethics and social conflicts.

The main function of social science fiction is to create awareness of where the world we inhabit is going. Although the author's objective is not moralistic in principle, the development of the themes and the way they are narrated are. Within this subgenre we are going to find three basic formats of telling these stories.
