Post-apocalyptic Sci-Fi

Get ready for the end of the world

Apocalyptic if they tell us about the great disaster that ruined most of humanity, and post-apocalyptic if what it shows us is how the survivors deal with the consequences. There are many ways to end humanity — or for humanity to end itself — but we will talk more about that another time.

It is clear that the human being can look for himself the apocalypse but hey, there is always the possibility that the sun engulfs us.
